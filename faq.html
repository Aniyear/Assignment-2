Frequently Asked Questions

~What are your restaurant's operating hours for breakfast, lunch, and dinner?

We're open to serve you throughout the day! Our breakfast hours are from 7:00 AM to 11:00 AM, followed by our lunch menu available from 11:30 AM to 3:00 PM. For dinner, we welcome you from 5:30 PM to 9:00 PM.

~Do you offer vegetarian and vegan options on your menu for all meals?

Absolutely! We understand the importance of catering to various dietary preferences. Our menu includes a wide range of vegetarian and vegan dishes for breakfast, lunch, and dinner, ensuring there's something for everyone.

~Can I order your products online?

The convenience of online ordering is just a click away. Browse our delectable selection and place your order from the comfort of your home. We're here to make your foodie dreams come true with the ease of a digital touch.

~Any Plans to Open a Shop Near My Area Soon?

While we may not be next door just yet, we're always exploring new locations to bring our delicious delights closer to you. Stay tuned for our expansion updates; you never know when we might be in your neighborhood!
